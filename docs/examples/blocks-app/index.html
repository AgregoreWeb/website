Use this as your own personal launcher. Add any links you wish to keep track of, and click on them to launch.

To start, click "Save Blocks". This will make your own IPNS based website saved to your Agregore browser which you can share with your friends. Blocks are automatically added to your site as you add them here.

Others can then visit your link on their own browsers and hit Save Blocks to start adding or removing links into an automatic copy of their own site.
